Pizza

Description

This Italian classic needs no introduction! Classic Margherita Pizza is a perfect amalgamation of cheesy, creamy sauce topped with a melange of spices and herbs. Baked to perfection, this classic Pizza can be prepared easily at home that too without putting in much efforts. For all those who believe that veggies break the 'cheesy' harmony of pizza, Classic Margherita Pizza is a delectable saviour. However, instead of going for the rubbery, store bought pizza, you can try this easy Margherita Pizza recipe at home and impress your loved one with your culinary skills. This amazing recipe was taken from times food which you can find here.

Ingredients

  • 1 large pizza base
  • 50 ml pizza sauce
  • 140 gm grated mozzarella
  • 60 gm sliced tomato
  • 2 gm basil
  • 1 gm salt
  • 2 ml olive oil pomace
  • 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven at 250 degree Celsius. Spread tomato ketchup, fresh basil along with grated mozzarella cheese at the top of pizza base, and garnish with sliced tomatoes.
  2. Spread tomato ketchup, fresh basil along with grated mozzarella cheese at the top of pizza base, and top it with sliced tomatoes.
  3. Bake in a pre-heated oven at 10 to 12 minutes and glaze with olive oil and basil.
  4. Serve hot when its done. Sprinkle some oregano and chilli flakes, and relish the taste of this yummy pizza!
